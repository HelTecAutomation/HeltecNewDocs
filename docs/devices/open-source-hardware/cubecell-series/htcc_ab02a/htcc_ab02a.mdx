## Product characteristics
- Arduino-Compatible, CE/FCC certified
- ASR605x + SX1262, LoRaWAN 1.0.2
- Ultra-low power, 3.5 µA deep sleep
- 1/2 AA battery + USB auto switch
- Long range & protection, CP2102 for programming/debugging
