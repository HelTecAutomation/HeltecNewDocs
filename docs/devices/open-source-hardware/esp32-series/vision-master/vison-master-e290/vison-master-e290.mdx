The Vision Master E290 (HT-VME290) is a 2.90-inch black-and-white E-Ink development kit with Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and LoRa connectivity, supporting multiple frameworks for applications like electronic labels, identity tags, and open-source projects such as Meshtastic.

  </a>
</div>}

## Product characteristics

- ESP32-S3R8 MCU, support Wi-Fi and BLE
- LoRa module is optional, compatible with Meshtastic
- Low power consumption, 18uA in deep sleep, continuous display for 180 days after power outage
- Onboard 2.90 inch 296 x 128 pixels display, support for partial refresh
- SH2.0-4P sensor interface is perfectly compatible with QuickLink series sensors
