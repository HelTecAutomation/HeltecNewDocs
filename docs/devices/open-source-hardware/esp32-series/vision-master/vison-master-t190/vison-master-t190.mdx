The Vision Master T190 is a highly integrated, multi-functional ESP32-S3 development kit with an optional LoRa communication module. It features a 1.9-inch TFT-LCD color display with a resolution of 170 × 320 pixels (RGB), capable of rendering up to 262,000 colors, delivering crisp, vibrant, and detailed visuals.

## Product characteristics
- ESP32-S3R8 MCU, support Wi-Fi and BLE
- LoRa module is optional, compatible with Meshtastic
- Low power consumption, 18uA in deep sleep
- Powerful display function, onboard 1.9-inch TFT-LCD display contains 170 (RGB)X320 dots and can display up to 262k colors
- SH2.0-4P sensor interface is compatible with QuickLink series sensors
